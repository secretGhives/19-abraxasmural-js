What about pricing & other?

After initial contact via email, we will proceed with the following steps;

  1. First Contact

    After brief introductions via email, we meet onsite to discuss the project further and get additional details about the scope of the work.

  2. Price

    Discussion about the budget and price agreement is achieved.

  3. Signed Contract & Design deposit

    Proposal for work which includes a contract is provided, agreement about payment methods and initial deposit of 10 is made, work on design begins.

  4. Design process & Sketch

    This stage depends on whether or not you have an idea or design or sketch ready, otherwise it's brainstorming session between parties until ideal composition is achieved.

  5. Final preparations & Mural Deposit

    Before work on site can begin, a deposit in the sum of 40 of total value is provided which is used to purchase required supplies to complete the job. This is the last point of design review before work begins.

  6. Painting

    Depending on the job site and agreement reached previous, painting will be done either in the early mornings due to sun exposure or late afternoon to late nights. Client can visit job site at anytime during process to comment and discuss progress with painter(s).

  7. Final Payment

    Job is complete, client has signed off on the work and remaining balance of remaining 50 is paid in full.
